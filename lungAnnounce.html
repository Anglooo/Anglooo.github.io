New EP 'Lungs' to be released 6-6-14 Posted 28

Our new EP 'Lungs' will be released on Friday 6th of June through Signal Flow Records. We will also release the music video for the lead track on the EP 'Sixth Sense' on the 6th of June. We're so excited to get this out as we have been working on this EP for just under a year alongside Aidan and JR from Signal Flow Records and we know it is by far the best release we have made to date. All artwork by Owen Wicksted
